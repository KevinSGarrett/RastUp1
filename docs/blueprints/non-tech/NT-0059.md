N\) Permissions, Buyer Trust & Privacy

Roles: Owner (billing/contracts), Editor (briefs/awards), Commenter
(notes/votes).

Payment Verified badge: once funding source on file.

Privacy: brand name masking until award; private addresses masked until
confirm; generalarea safety share during active booking only.

O\) Safety & Quality Controls

Eligibility gates: RFP to IDVerified (Trustedonly optional).

Budget floors: prevent unrealistic budgets; assist with suggested
ranges.

Offplatform detection: warnings & throttles in chat.

NSFW guardrails: artisticnudity only; explicit content blocked; nudity
tiers & safety addendum enforced.

Buyer reputation: Providers see Buyer Rep (ontime payment, dispute rate,
repeat bookings).

P\) Pricing & Credits (Launch)

Smart Invite: free.

RFP Broadcast credits: 5 for \$29 (consumed per role/city broadcast).

Foundingcity promo: 5 free RFP credits for new PaymentVerified buyers +
0% buyer fee on first booking.

Credit refunds: autocredit 1 if \<3 qualified responses by deadline;
full pack credit if 0 responses.

Q\) Integrations & Contracts

Escrow & payouts: per §1.3 (milestones supported; partial releases on
partial accept).

Smart Docs: autoattached on award; esign gating before checkin;
PDFs/versioning in thread.

Calendar: ICS export; conflict checks vs provider availability.

Storage: external delivery required for large assets; platform stores
previews + manifests only (see §1.4).

Social OAuth: for autoverification of influence deliverables.

R\) Analytics

Buyer dashboard: outreach→response, timetoaward, spend by project, cost
per deliverable, provider performance.

Platform: RFP adoption, response quality, award rate, GMV per brief,
credit refund rate, Instant Book vs RFP split, deliverable dispute
rates, autoaccept rate.

S\) KPIs & Targets (first 90 days/city)

Adoption: ≥ 35% of bookings originate via Smart Invite/RFP flows.

Speed: median brief → first qualified response ≤ 2 hours (business
hours); brief → award ≤ 48 hours for Quick Briefs.

Quality: ≥ 60% of broadcasts yield ≥3 qualified responses; provider
response rate ≥ 45%; buyer CSAT ≥ 4.5/5.

Trust: disputes/noshows at or below Instant Book baseline; offplatform
attempts in RFP threads \< 1%.

Economics: RFPsourced GMV ≥ 30% by month 6; credit refund rate \< 12%.

Delivery health: autoaccept ≥ 50% of jobs; deliverable disputes \< 5%;
influence noncompliance \< 6%.

Influence verification (OAuth/URL snapshot) with posting window &

Attendance & disputes wired (checkin/out, cure window, partial refunds,

Boards & collaboration, Concierge Light, and Human Concierge pilot live.

Buyer roles & privacy, Payment Verified badge, NSFW guardrails.

Analytics dashboards shipping KPIs above.

Clear scope → fewer disputes: Smart Docs + normalized quotes + manifests
make expectations explicit.

Storage costs contained: We host previews & paperwork; originals stay in
Drive/Dropbox/etc. with verifiable manifests.

Faster to "yes": Smart Invite + Quick Quotes + Fit Score reduce time to
award.

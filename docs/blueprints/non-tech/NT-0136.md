B\) Document Types & Catalog

Legal/Operational docs (rendered as PDF & HTML):

SOW (Statement of Work) --- per role, roleaware fields
(talent/photog/videographer/creator) + LBG aware.

Model Release / Talent Release --- 18+ only; IDverified signers; alias
display but legal name on signature page.

IP & License Addenda --- commercial/editorial usage, term, geography,
exclusivity, buyout options.

House Rules (Studio) --- access, usage, equipment, noise, parking,
deposit grounds.

Safety Addenda --- chaperone policy, emergency contact, areashare
consent, code of conduct.

Change Orders --- schedule/price adjustments midthread.

Refund/Delivery Acknowledgements --- standardized acceptance/refund
confirmations.

Tax Consent Texts --- edelivery consent language (links with
§4.12/§4.14.7).

Messaging templates (transactional & lifecycle):

Checkout/escrow, acceptance, reminders, reschedule, deliverables
due/accepted, revision prompts, dispute opened/closed, payout
queued/sent, 1099 available, verification nudges, city launch,
promotions.

C\) Clause Library & Safe Variables

Clause blocks:

Atomic, reusable paragraphs with conditions (role, city, studio, NSFW
state, presence of deposit, licensing tier).

Tags: #legal, #ops, #safety, #tax, #studio.

Deprecation with automigration suggestions (never changes older, pinned
documents).

Variable catalog (strongly typed & validated):

Examples: {{booking.start_datetime_local}}, {{booking.city}},
{{party.buyer.display_name}}, {{party.seller.legal_name_masked}},
{{pricing.subtotal}}, {{license.scope}}, {{studio.deposit_cap}}.

Validators: required/optional, regex, min/max, enum; preview with sample
data before publish.

Forbidden variables in public surfaces (email/phone/payment handles) are
blocked.

D\) Template Editor UI

Block editor (left: clause library, right: doc outline).

Conditions panel to include/exclude clauses by role/city/flags.

Variables inspector with type checks and autofill suggestions.

Diff viewer (before/after) + impact preview on 10 sample bookings.

Version header: author, reason code, linked Jira/issue, publish window
(start/end), scopes (global/state/city/role).

Rollback button restores prior version in one click (future bookings
only).

E\) Versioning, Approvals & Publishing

States: Draft → Legal Review → Ops Review → Scheduled/Published →
Archived.

Approvals: Legal must approve legal doc changes; Marketing approves
comms; Ops approves city addenda.

Scope & scheduling: publish per city/role/cohort with local timezone;
staged rollout (1%→10%→100%).

Pinning: each booking pins doc versions at create (SOW/House Rules) and
accept (releases/license).

Audit: immutable log with actor, reason, diffs, affected scopes, and
rollback link.

F\) ESignatures & Records (Docs)

Signature methods: typedname + checkbox + timestamp, or embedded esign
capture (hosted by provider).

Signer identity: ties to platform account; legal name private, shown
only on signature page and receipts.

Cosigners: both sides sign; LBG: talent/studio sign their respective
docs.

Records: render to PDF, hash & store, link from booking and receipts;
download available to both parties.

18+ enforcement: release templates block minor language; ID Verified
required to sign talent releases.

B\) Thread Types & Routing

Inquiry thread (prebooking) --- starts from profile/search/brief invite.
Uses DM credit on the first outbound message only; replies are free.

Booking thread (Talent) --- created on acceptance/IB; status chips:
Booked ‚Üí In progress ‚Üí Delivered ‚Üí Completed; shows escrow status.

Booking thread (Studio) --- created on acceptance/IB; shows access
details (masked until esign), deposit hold state, checkin/out status.

RFP thread --- buyer broadcast ‚Üí provider reply; converts to
Inquiry/Booking on award.

LBG umbrella --- not a chat; a summary bar linking to the two booking
threads; supports Group Broadcast (see ¬ßI).

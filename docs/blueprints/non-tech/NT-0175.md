§5.8 --- Data Quality & Testing

Goal: Guarantee that everything built on our data---dashboards, payouts,
taxes, safety actions, experiments---rests on correct, fresh,
privacysafe tables. Detect issues early, quarantine safely, block
sensitive outputs when needed, and make fixes auditable and repeatable.

A\) Scope & Objectives

Where we test: SDK/Edge → Bus → Bronze → Silver → Gold → Semantic layer
(metrics) → BI/Exports.

What we test: Contracts, IDs & referential integrity, business
invariants, money & reconciliations, freshness & completeness, privacy &
masking, anomaly baselines.

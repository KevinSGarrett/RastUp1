B\) Layout (Desktop & Mobile)

Desktop

Left: Filter drawer (collapsible).

Center: Results list (cards) with infinite scroll.

Right: Optional map toggle (pins show price or a role icon).

Top: Role/Listing selector tabs (Model • Photographer • Videographer •
Creator • FanSub Creator • Studio), City chip, Date/Time pill, Save
Search.

Mobile

Role tabs at top; Filters opens fullscreen; map toggles within results.

Sticky bar presents Filters, Save, and (for talent with date/time)
Attach a Studio pill.

Skeletons render immediately; results stream in batches.

C\) Role/Listing Selector & Gating

Tabs: People (Model • Photographer • Videographer • Creator • FanSub
Creator) and Places (Studio) separated by a small label.

Safe Mode ON hides FanSubscription Creator and People results with
nudity tiers beyond "Lingerie/Swim."

Switching to Studio shows Studiospecific filters and never reveals
explicit content.

D\) Filters

D1) Common (People & Studios)

City & radius (mi/km)

Date/time window (+ duration for talent packages that require it)

Budget / Price (People: package price; Studio: price/hr)

Distance from city center or buyer's set location

Availability first (toggle)

Minimum Reputation Score slider

Verification toggles: ID Verified only, Trusted Pro only (People),
Verified Studio only (Studios)

D2) People --- Model / Creator (nonFanSub)

Look: height, hair color, eye color, skin tone, tattoos/piercings (Y/N)

Measurements (bust/waist/hips or chest/waist/hips; numeric ranges)

Genres/Comfort: fashion, beauty, fitness, lingerie/swim, artistic nude
(Y/N), chaperone friendly (Y/N)

Deliverables (for UGC creators): shortform video, stills, usage window,
turnaround

Travel: willing to travel (Y/N), touring dates (city list)

D3) People --- Photographer

Specialties: portrait, editorial, product, beauty, lifestyle

Deliverables: number of edited images, RAW delivery (Y/N), turnaround
SLA

Licensing tiers: personal, brand organic, paid ads (checkboxes)

Instant Book (toggle)

(Intentionally exclude gear/software; users don't care at selection
time.)

D4) People --- Videographer

Formats: talkinghead, product broll, event, music video, shortform

Deliverables: length/resolution, color grade, sound mix, captions

Drone license (Y/N), Instant Book toggle

D5) People --- FanSubscription Creator (visible only when Safe Mode OFF)

Platforms offered (OnlyFans, Fansly, Patreon)

Promo types: feed post, story, duet/stitch, link in bio (duration),
collab shoot

Posting window & minlive

Audience geography (top countries) (optional Phase2)

D6) Studio (Places)

Price/hr (min--max), Min hours, Capacity, Size

Creative amenities: Natural light (orientation), Cyc wall (+
dimensions), Blackout, ceiling height ≥ N, soundtreated,
makeup/dressing, grip/lighting available, parking/loadin, A/C,
kitchenette, shower

Rules: Instant Book only; Verified Studio only

Schedule: date/time slot selector; bufferfriendly windows

E\) Social Metrics Panel (People roles; perplatform targeting)

Platforms: IG • YT • TT • X (launch).

Filters per platform: Followers (min--max), Engagement Rate (min %), Avg
views (YT/TT), 30/90day growth (min %).

Logic: Match ANY (default) or Match ALL selected platforms.

Verification: Verifiedonly (default) uses connectedaccount metrics;
toggle Include selfreported to expand results (unverified numbers are
labeled).

Freshness: nightly sync; user can Refresh metrics up to 3×/day
platformwide (ratelimited).

Explainers: tooltip "Followers ≠ engagement" + definitions of ER per
platform.

Cards show compact chips like IG 182k • 2.8% ER / TT 520k • 110k avg
views with a tiny verified tick when sourced via API.

Addendum A ยง2 GotoMarket: Studios Launch Scope & Emphasis
(ResearchUpdated)

Goal: Decide how (or if) "Studios" appears at launch without diluting
focus or overcompeting with Peerspace. Preserve our core: talent
discovery & bookings. Use studios as a complementary, lowfriction addon
that increases conversion for shoots---not a coequal vertical on day
one.

A\) Launch Positioning (what "Studios" means at MVP)

Name & framing: "Verified Studios (Lite)" --- curated creative spaces
and private studios suitable for photo/video shoots (not
events/parties).

Role in v1: Complementary to talent bookings. Studios appear as an
attachable option during briefs/checkout and as a subhub under Browse,
not a topnav pillar.

Geographic scope: Founding city only (e.g., Houston), seed 15--30
quality studios max; expand after PMF signals.

B\) Why not a full "Peerspace competitor" at launch

Focus & liquidity: Early marketplaces win by narrowing scope. A full
venue marketplace doubles complexity (supply ops, insurance, deposits,
map/amenity search, etc.).

Differentiation: Our edge is talent + tools (briefs, deliverables,
social metrics). Studios should amplify those flows (shoot readiness),
not become the main show.

Quality \> quantity: A curated set of creativeready spaces beats a thin
longtail that invites comparison to mature incumbents.

F\) Marketplace Mechanics (how studios add conversion, not complexity)

Brief uplift: when buyer composes a shoot brief, Attach a Studio offers
3 nearby matches with "creative amenities" chips; increases award rate
by reducing buyer friction.

Talent crosssell: photographers who own a studio can toggle Studio AddOn
(hourly) inside their package; the system then exposes that studio at
checkout.

Boards: saved boards can include both talent & studios; Compare view
keeps them separated by type.

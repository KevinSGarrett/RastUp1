H\) Beta KPIs (exit checklist)

Liquidity: Search→Inquiry ≥ 20%; Inquiry→Booking ≥ 35% (Houston).

Trust: IDverified share ≥ 60%; Trusted Pro ≥ 15% of active providers;
safety reports ≤ 0.2 / 1,000 legs.

Payments: ≥ 20% of checkouts via ACH; finance close on time 14/14 days;
recon variance ≤ 0.5%.

Promotions pilot: ≥ 15% eligible providers try Boost; median ROAS ≥
2.5×; invalidclick \< 7%.

Social metrics: ≥ 40% of active providers connected to ≥1 platform; ≥
30% of searches use social filters.

Engagement: ≥ 25% of buyers create a Board; ≥ 30% of providers post at
least once/month.

If 2+ critical KPIs miss after remediation, hold GA and iterate.

I\) Risks & Mitigations (Betaspecific)

Quota/API limits on social platforms → cache & stale badges;
verifiedonly toggle defaults ON.

Promotions perceived as paytowin → labels, caps, eligibility, and
holdoutbased incrementality reporting.

ACH disputes/returns → clear copy; returns automation; ACH only for
lowrisk cohorts initially.

Background check anxiety → optin + incentives (fee nudge, Boost credit);
fast vendor SLAs; privacy copy.

Tax calc edge cases → city allowlist + automatic fallbacks; Admin
override + nightly reconciliation.

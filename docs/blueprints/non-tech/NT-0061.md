C\) Package Templates & AddOns (per role)

Templates are opinionated defaults sellers can edit---keeps quotes
comparable & reduces scope creep.

Photographer

Starter Session (60--90 min): X looks, X edited photos (e.g., 10), raw
previews for selection, 7day delivery, 1 revision.

HalfDay / FullDay: timebased; includes lighting, basic studio backdrop;
deliverables by count (e.g., 25/50 edits).

Campaign Shoot: team coordination, shot list, licensing preset
(web/social/paid ads tiers).

Videographer

ShortForm: 1--3 vertical edits ≤60s; footage capture + edit; licensed
music option.

Product Video: 30--60s hero + cutdowns; basic motion graphics; color &
sound pass.

Event Coverage: hours + deliverable minutes, highlight & social reels.

Model

Lookbook / Ecomm: rate + number of looks; posing & outfit changes; usage
preset (web/social).

Creative/Editorial: half/full day; boundaries & chaperone policy
surfaced.

UGC Package: selfshot stills/videos per brief + Influence addons
(posts/stories).

Creator (Influence)

IG Post + Story Pack, TikTok Post + Duet/Stitch, YouTube Short +
Community Post with posting window & min live duration (autoverified per
§3.5).

Addons (crossrole): extra edits/looks, rush 48h, studio addon (price per
hour), HMUA partner, assistant, BTS content, extended licensing, raw
file addon (limited), travel matrix tiers, overtime (per 30m).

D\) Availability, Calendar & Touring

Instant Book windows: sellers choose days/times eligible for IB; buffers
enforced; travel time autocalc by radius.

Blackouts & holds: dragtoblock; softhold expires after X hours if buyer
doesn't escrow.

Calendar sync: ICS oneway export; ICS imports supported
(Google/Apple/Outlook).

Touring calendar: add cities with date ranges; appears as "Visiting
\[City\]" chip in search; availability filters respect touring.

Reschedule & edits (providerside): providers can propose time/date
changes, price adjustments, partial refunds---buyer must approve
inthread; Smart Docs autoamend and receipts reissue (per §1.4 addendum).

E\) Inbox & Lead Pipeline (built on §1.4 Messaging)

Pipeline states: New → Needs Info → Quoted → Awarded → Booked →
Delivered → Completed → Archived.

Action bar: Quick Reply templates, Quick Quote, send availability poll,
propose calendar slots, add Zoom link (if remote briefing).

Snooze/remind: Snooze thread for 2h/24h/3d; autoremind to follow up on
unanswered invites.

Autonudge: once/day per thread (caps per §1.4) with lastsent preview.

Ratelimit & DM credits: credits meter with +20 for ID Verified; +20 for
Trusted Pro (max 80 at launch, per §3.2).

Offplatform guardrails: gentle warnings & throttles if payment/handle
sharing detected.

F\) Pricing Intelligence & Controls

City benchmark & pricefit index: shows how package price compares to
city/role median; flag "too low/too high" risk.

Lastminute fill: toggle % discount for slots \<72h away to lift
utilization.

Intro discount: automatic for first 3 onplatform bookings.

Coupons: provider can generate singleuse or timeboxed codes (stacking
rules respect §3.1 fee floors).

Dynamic addons visibility: surface popular addons postaward (e.g., "Rush
delivery -- add \$XX") with singleclick change orders.

Travel matrix editor: zone/mileage tiers, permile or perzone; autoquote
at checkout; postevent Travel Adjustment workflow (evidence, 48h cure).

G\) Smart Docs & Policy Controls (sellerfriendly)

Template library: editable clauses for revisions (#, scope), change
orders, overtime, kill fees, licensing tiers (web/social/paid/term).

Preset packs by vertical (ecomm, editorial, music video, UGC).

Chaperone & boundaries (models/creators): defaults propagate into SOW &
Safety Addendum; booking brief enforces buyer acknowledgment.

License calculator: price deltas for media types/geos/terms; add to
quote with one click.

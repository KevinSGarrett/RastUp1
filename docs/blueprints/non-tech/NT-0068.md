5\) Verification loop (trust → visibility → bookings)

Completing ID unlocks Instant Book + DM credits → higher
inquiry→booking.

Trusted Pro (BG) grants fee nudge, RFP priority, Boost coupon → more
awards → more reviews → Rep Score up → ranking tiebreaks.

D\) Retention Features (per user type)

Sellers

Lead Inbox nudges: "Reply within 2h to keep Fast Responder badge."

Utilization alerts: "You have 4 open IB slots this weekend; enable
LastMinute Fill for +18% leads."

Smart "Raise price" hints when win rate \>70% and price well below city
median.

Touring assist: suggest nearby cities and dates based on follower
heatmap; autocreate "Visiting \[City\]" chip.

Buyers

Shortlist intelligence: "3 of your saved photogs now Instant Book next
Friday."

Availability windows: "Top models in your board opened 8--10pm Fri."

Budget guardrails: recommendations to adjust range for ≥3 qualified
responses.

"Book again" reminders synced to last project cadence.

E\) Referrals & Credits (twosided, abuseresistant)

Provider invites provider: both get +20 DM credits after invitee
publishes a package and completes 1 booking.

Buyer invites buyer: inviter gets 1 RFP credit when invitee posts a
brief and awards a booking.

Crossside referrals: if a provider invites a buyer who books them, both
get \$15 buyer credit toward next booking (used on buyer fee only).

Guardrails: peruser monthly caps; ID required to redeem; same device/IP
fraud detection; clawback if first booking is refunded for fraud.

F\) Saved Searches, Alerts & Digests

Saved search alerts: new matches batched 1/day/search; "turn off for 30
days" easy toggle.

Weekly city digest (optin): featured case studies, Verified Studios with
open slots, "Top rising creators" (Rep + recent growth).

Postbooking digest: 7 days after completion---review reminder, case
study prompt, "book again" CTA.

G\) Follows, Favorites & Boards (organization that retains)

Follow counts on profiles; Following tab shows only updates from
accounts you follow.

Favorites folders/Boards: draganddrop; status tags autoupdate
(Invited/Proposed/Awarded).

Share boards externally (readonly) with optional NDA gate; activity
pings when providers on a board go Verified or open IB slots.

H\) Notifications & Frequency Caps

Channels: inapp, email, push (optin), SMS (critical only).

Global caps: max 2 push/day, 3 email/week by default; digest merges
alerts.

User controls: pertopic toggles (Saved searches, Boards, Touring, Case
studies, Instant Book windows).

Quiet hours: localtime 10pm--8am (nonurgent push deferred).

Respect Safe Mode: 18+ gated content never appears in public emails;
only visible inapp with toggle ON.

I\) WinBack & Churn Save

Risk cohort (14 days idle): send "quick action" emails ("Publish 1
package template in 60s" / "Send 3 Smart Invites from your board").

Churn cohort (45 days idle): offer \$15 buyer credit or +20 DM credits
if they complete a booking within 14 days.

Exit survey on deactivation: reasons (price, low leads, complexity,
safety). Autocreate remedial tasks (e.g., "Enable ID to unlock IB").

Pause mode: let users pause discovery (hide from search) without
deleting; set autoresume.

J\) Feed & Posting (roleaware, one post → many views)

One composer with role tags; viewers filter by role.

Post types: Case study, Availability card, Touring card, "Looking
for..." (LFM/LFP rolegated), Verified promo delivered (SFW preview).

Ranking: follows → samecity trending → recommended (Repweighted).

Moderation: AI + human; artisticonly policy; Safe Mode hides 18+ until
toggled.

A\) Global search flow (unchanged)

Step 1 (required): pick a role to search (Models / Photographers /
Videographers / Creators).

Step 2: rolespecific filters appear; result card is a Service Profile
(SP) with role label, rating, starting price, highlights, verification,
social metrics (if verified), and Instant Book badge (if enabled).

B\) Rolespecific filters (final list)

Global filters (all roles): Location+radius, Available on (date/time),
Budget (range), Rating & \# reviews, Verification (ID / Background /
SocialVerified), Response time, Completion rate, Instant Book,
Languages, Travel willing, Last active.

Models --- "find the look + reliability + reach"

Look: height; measurements (bust/chest, waist, hips; cup optin);
dress/clothing; shoe; body type; tattoos; piercings; hair
(color/length); eye color; skin tone; facial hair (if applicable); age
(18+ only); ethnicity (selfdescribed).

Work & logistics: categories (fashion, beauty, fitness, swim, artistic
nude); base rate (hour/day); travel radius/fee; chaperonefriendly;
release preference.

Reliability: reply time, ontime rate, noshow rate.

Social (if connected): platform(s), followers range, engagement rate,
30day growth, audience geos.

Photographers --- "style, output, speed, price"

Style/specialty: portrait, fashion/editorial, beauty, commercial,
product, events; style descriptors (natural light, cinematic, highkey,
moody, colortrue).

Deliverables: edited image count, RAW provided?, lookbook/ecomm sets;
turnaround; studio access (yes/no); preferred shoot type
(studio/outdoor).

Rates: hour/day, packages.

Reliability: completion, ontime, rating.

Social (if connected): followers, engagement, recent growth.

Videographers --- "format, deliverables, speed, price"

Types: reels/shorts, promo, music video, event, interview, product.

Deliverables: runtime(s), aspect (vertical/horizontal), revisions, sound
capture, captions.

Turnaround, rate, travel.

Style: snappy/reels, cinematic, docu, productclean.

Reliability & social (as above).

Creators/Influencers --- "reach that matches goals"

Platforms: IG/TikTok/YouTube (multiselect).

Followers, engagement rate, avg views, story views (if available).

Audience: top countries/cities; age/gender split (if available).

Niches: beauty, fitness, cosplay, gaming, fashion, etc.

Deliverables & rates: grid post, stories pack, reel/short, live,
bundles; lead time.

Growth (30/90d); prior brand collabs; reliability (ontime posting rate),
rating.

Note: these social filters are enabled when a user has granted the
platform access via Instagram Graph / YouTube / TikTok APIs; otherwise
results can include Unverified stats only if the buyer toggles them on.

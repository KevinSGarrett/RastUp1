A\) Policy Scope & Definitions (Studios)

Studio = Space Listing (placebased). Trust controls apply to the space,
not a person's role.

Host = Account owner of a Studio listing.

Buyer = The party renting the studio (may be a brand, photographer, or
creator).

Artisticonly policy applies to all bookings: onplatform bookings may
include artistic nudity; explicit sexual content/acts are prohibited (no
adult content production facilitated or booked through the platform).

B\) What We Verify (Studio Lite)

Address & Ownership/RighttoRent: government/utility/lease doc + name
match; geocode/place match.

Basic Safety Disclosures: max occupancy, egress, rules summary (noise,
strobe, haze, open flame, ladders).

Host Identity: host must be IDVerified; background checks (Trusted Pro)
remain peoplerole only.

Badge: passing shows Verified Studio.

Note: Studio Lite is not a building inspection or insurance substitute;
it's a light authenticity & disclosure check.

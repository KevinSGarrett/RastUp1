D\) Safety Features (product)

D1. Safer Discovery

Filters: "ID Verified," "Trusted Pro," "SocialVerified," minimum rating,
low cancellation rate, response time.

Badges on Results: Always visible with short tooltips.

Profile Signals: Response time, completion rate, punctuality rate,
dispute rate (bucketed).

D2. Safer Booking & Meetup

Builtin contracts (booking agreement, model release, usage license)
autoattached and esigned.

Checkin/Checkout timestamps (optional); shared generalarea pin (50--150m
fuzzing) for privacy.

Trusted Contact (optional): share booking summary + checkin/out signals
with a private contact via secure link.

Location Tips: Premeeting banner: meet in public places or verified
studios for firsttime collaborations.

D3. Messaging Protections

Read receipts and last active to set expectations.

Automoderation for: slurs/harassment, sexual solicitations, payment
offplatform, contact info spam → inline warnings + throttles on repeat.

Block & Report (one tap) with categories and freetext; immediate mute on
both sides.

D4. Safety Flag & Triage

Prominent Safety Flag in booking threads.

Highpriority queue for Trust & Safety with SLA (e.g., triage within 2
business hours; faster during shoots).

When flagged: temporarily disable accused user's Instant Book and Smart
Invites until triage completes.

D5. Privacy Controls

Email/phone hidden by default; reveal toggle after booking (optional).

Media visibility: NSFW visibility requires optin; viewer must be 18+ and
ID Verified to view.

Anonymized handles available for public browsing (real name optional on
profile; legal name in contracts only).

E\) Moderation & Content Policy

E1. Upload Rules

No explicit sexual acts; no minors; no doxxing; no hate imagery; no
illegal items.

NSFW toggle required where applicable; autodetection queues borderline
content for human review.

E2. Review Integrity

Reviews allowed only after completed bookings.

Noshow participants can still be reviewed with a system label ("Noshow
reported by counterparty").

Reports against reviews trigger moderation (defamation, irrelevance,
hate). Uphold facts tied to bookings.

E3. Enforcement Ladder

Education & warning

Temporary feature limits (DM caps, Instant Book disabled)

Timeboxed suspension

Removal and device/account ban

Strike policy: late cancels, noshows, policy violation content/messages;
expiry after X months.

F\) Fraud, Risk & Account Integrity

Risk scoring at signup and on each transaction: device fingerprint, IP
geolocation, velocity checks, mismatched card/name signals.

KYC for payouts: ID Verified + bank account name matching.

Hold & reserves: Higher reserve or payout delay for new/untrusted
accounts, relaxing with reputation.

Duplicate accounts / ban evasion: Device & identity correlation; block
on detection.

Chargeback defense: Autoassemble evidence packet (contracts, chat,
checkins, deliverables logs).

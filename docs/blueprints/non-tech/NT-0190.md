E\) Data & Analytics (Phase0 minimum)

Streaming Silver tables: booking_events, fin_events, events_search,
events_msg.

Gold facts: fact_booking_leg, fact_payment(+lines), fact_payout(+legs).

Ops tiles: search→request, request→accept, accept→complete, refund rate,
payout latency (median).

Freshness SLO: \<15 min for ops; daily close by 06:00 local.

Basic DQ tests: charge lines sum; allocations match; orphan legs=0;
payout sums match lines.

F\) Copy, Policy & Safety (Phase0)

Artisticnudityonly banner on portfolio uploader; public SafeMode always
on for guests; toggle for signedin users 18+.

Safety tips automessage before first meeting; trusted contact optional
field (notify on start/finish).

Contracts: standard SOW + model release templates via Smart Docs v0;
PDFs stored 7 years (hash recorded).

Noshow and late cancel outcomes mapped to refund engine; strike counter
recorded (no public badge yet).

G\) Operational Runbooks (must exist)

Refunds & Changes: how support selects policy band, applies partial
refunds, and reissues receipts.

Escrow & Payouts: what to do on 3DS failure, payout failure, reserve
queries.

IDV: handling failed verifications and manual overrides with reasons.

Disputes: evidence checklist (chat, manifest, release PDF), timeline,
and guidance.

Incident: payments outage, tax outage (Phase0 tax mostly off), messaging
outage.

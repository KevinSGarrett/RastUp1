3\) About (rolespecific fields)

Model / Creator quick facts: height, measurements, hair, eyes, skin
tone, tattoos/piercings (Y/N), shoe/dress sizes; Comfort levels: genres
(fashion, fitness, lingerie/swim, artistic nude yes/no), chaperone
policy, travel radius; languages.

Photographer: specialties (portrait, editorial, product, beauty,
events), deliverables (edits count), turnaround SLA, licensing tiers,
team size (if any), travel policy. (Gear is optional, deprioritized.)

Videographer: specialties (talkinghead, shortform, product),
deliverables (length/resolution), color/sound/post options, drone
license (Y/N), music licensing policy.

FanSubscription Creator (18+ gated): supported platforms (OnlyFans,
Fansly, Patreon), promo deliverables (e.g., story frames, feed posts,
duet/stitch), posting windows & minlive (from §3.5), whitelisting/usage
rules (brand usage, duration).

Socials: verified connections (IG/YT/TT/X) + icons; follower and ER/avg
views chips; linkonly platforms marked Unverified.

Languages, travel & touring cities upcoming (optional calendar).

4\) Reviews

Verified booking reviews (rolescoped) at top; Community references below
(lower weight).

Sort: Most recent • Highest • Lowest.

Review breakdown with charts (quality, professionalism, reliability).

Owner responses allowed (single reply per review).

Reputation "Why" panel: contribution bars (reviews/volume 40%,
reliability 25%, responsiveness 10%, verifications 15%, recent 10%;
penalties decay).

5\) Updates (profile posts/feed)

Short posts, WIP photos, behindthescenes (SFW).

Followers see these posts in their Home feed; Follow count visible on
profile.

Pinned update supported (one per role).

Tag collaborators; approvals required as with Portfolio.

6\) Policies & FAQs

Cancellation (role's matrix summary), Reschedule, Deliverables &
revisions, Travel fees, Chaperone, Boundaries & comfort, Safety tips.

Links to KB for deeper policy detail.

D\) Booking & Messaging Entry Points (from profile)

Primary CTA: Instant Book (if IDverified & enabled) or Request to Book
(approval flow).

Secondary CTA: Message (thread; prefilled context from the package or
profile).

Add to Board: saves card to a shortlist board.

Attach a Studio: appears in checkout, not on profile; profiles with a
linked studio show Has Studio chip linking to the studio page.

E\) Owner View (editor) per Role

Editor shell: /seller/profile/{role} with tabs matching public:
Portfolio, Packages, About, Reviews, Updates, Policies, Analytics

Portfolio (owner)

Dragreorder; set covers; set privacy; approve/decline tags; watermark
toggle; alttext fields; SafeMode warnings for borderline content.

Packages & Addons

Create/edit packages (templates), addons, license presets; Instant Book
toggle (gated by ID), Travel fee config (zone/mi).

About

Role fields editor; nudity tier toggle (artistic nude Y/N for
models/creators); chaperone policy; languages; touring schedule.

Availability

Calendar (IB windows/buffers), ICS sync, touring cities; block dates.

Policies

Perrole micropolicies (cancellation windows, reschedule rules within
platform bands), deliverables SLA, revision rounds.

Updates

Compose posts (text + image/video), tag collaborators, pin/unpin; post
scheduler (optional).

Reviews

Readonly list + reply; request a Community reference link (offplatform
collaborator may submit; owner approves).

B\) Deliverables & Acceptance Tests (DoD)

Social metrics in search

Filters: perplatform IG/YT/TT/X followers (min--max), ER %, avg views,
30/90day growth; Verifiedonly toggle.

Cards show chips: e.g., IG 182k • 2.8% ER with "Verified by platform"
tooltip.

DoD: nightly sync + 3×/day manual refresh; stale badge after 48h;
queries respect ANY/ALL logic; verifiedonly removes selfreported stats.

ACH payments

Add/verify bank (Plaidstyle); buyerfee reduces to 3% when using ACH;
clear banner.

DoD: failure/fallback path to card; receipts show method and fee math;
finance close reconciles ACH returns.

Promotions pilot

Search Boost (CPC) with city×role floors; Featured carousels (weekly
flat).

Caps: ≤2 promoted in top 20, ≤1 above the fold; perbuyer frequency caps;
"Promoted" label + "Why am I seeing this?"

Eligibility: ID Verified, Rep ≥70, no active strikes, query match.

DoD: spend dashboard (impr, clicks, CPC, inquiries, bookings, ROAS), 10%
holdout for incrementality, invalidclick autocrediting, QS ordering
within paid slots only.

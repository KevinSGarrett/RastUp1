E\) FoundingCity Perks & Budgets

Perks (config via §4.14.2)

Buyer fee holiday (e.g., 0% buyer fee) --- windowed

Provider fee nudge (e.g., −2% for 60 days) --- capped by cohort

Verification subsidies: first N ID or Background passes funded

Boost starter credits: \$15 on Trusted Pro pass (once)

Budgets

Tiles: allocated vs spent vs slated; alerts at 80/100%.

(§4.14.6).

Eligibility

Enforce: id_verified && !recent_strike (and Trusted Pro where
applicable).

Cityscoped caps per user (e.g., max \$30 in credits/30 days).

F\) "Top in {City}" Rotations & Merchandising

Carousels: "Top Models in Houston", "...Photographers...",
"...Videographers...", "...Creators...", "Verified Studios".

Rotation logic: weighted roundrobin among eligible profiles (Rep ≥
threshold, IB on, recent positive reviews).

Fairness caps: max X impressions/week per profile; enforce diversity (no
duplicates in adjacent tiles).

Manual pin (rare): Ops can pin up to 2 slots for timeboxed campaigns
(record reason; autounpin).

A/B: carousel order and labels (e.g., "Available this week" vs "Instant
Book").

G\) Studio Seeding & Coverage

Neighborhood heatmap with target coverage; shows Verified Studios and
peak booking windows.

Studio outreach cohort builder (amenities needed: cyclorama, natural
light, dressing rooms, parking).

Deposit defaults & House Rules templates pulled from §4.14.10; autoapply
on onboarding.

Studio perks: reduced Featured price for first 4 weeks; extra visibility
in LBG flows.

Quality gate: minimum photo set + safety disclosures; Studio Verified
required to appear in checkout selector.

H\) Demand Seeding (Brands, Agencies, SMBs)

Brand prospecting: import CRM lists; tag by vertical
(fashion/beauty/fitness/real estate).

RFP Broadcast rules: prebuilt briefs per vertical; budget presets; city
target; send to Verified + Rep ≥ 70 supply.

Buyer incentives: firstbooking credits, ACH fee discount callouts;
concierge assist flag.

Casestudy pipeline: select completed bookings → request permission →
publish (adds to city landing page).

I\) Local Events: "Collab Days" & Tours

Event builder: schedule Collab Day at a Verified Studio (capacity, time
slots, optin roles).

RSVP & waitlist: eligibility (ID Verified), slot allocation, reminders;
optional nominal fee to reduce noshows.

Touring calendar: surface "Visiting talent" weeks; suggest Smart Invites
to local buyers/providers.

Afteraction: collect references; feature top outcomes in city carousels.

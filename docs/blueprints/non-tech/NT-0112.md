D\) Person --- ID Verification Flow

Route: /verify/id → 4step wizard

About you --- legal name, DOB, country/state; explain "Your public
profile can use an alias; legal name is private."

Document capture --- front/back (DL/ID/Passport); autoOCR; MRZ when
available.

Selfie liveness --- short movement prompts; face match score presented
as "passed/needs review."

Review & consent --- purpose, retention policy, and submit.

UX states:

Instant result: Passed → badge applied immediately.

Needs review: manual review SLA ≤ 24 h; user can continue using
platform; IB remains gated until pass.

Fail: give concrete next steps (better lighting, correct doc type) and
allow 2 more attempts before manual review.

Privacy: Store only required vectors/metadata; no raw images shown to
other users; legal name never public unless user opts in.

E\) Person --- Background Check (Trusted Pro)

Route: /verify/background → compliant consent flow

Consent & disclosure: explain scope; this is not employment, but we
follow FCRAstyle notices.

PII capture: SSN (masked in UI), address history (5 years), legal name
variations.

Run checks: national/criminal sex offender, relevant county/state,
global sanctions (where legal), identity verification.

Results taxonomy: Clear, Review, Consider with reason codes.

Adverse action (riskmanaged):

Preadverse notice with report copy & dispute instructions.

7day response window (configurable); investigation pause.

Final decision recorded; detailed internal notes; user notified.

Policy highlights:

Disqualifying offenses scoped to platform safety (e.g., violent crimes,
stalking, trafficking).

Recency & rehabilitation considered (timeweighted); expunged/sealed
records ignored where required.

Recheck cadence: every 12 months or upon safety trigger.

Cost: passthrough pricing with foundingcity subsidies (§3.4); status
shows any coupon applied.

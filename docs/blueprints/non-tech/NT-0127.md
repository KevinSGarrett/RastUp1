C\) Payouts, Reserves & Early Payout

Payout batches

Views: Queued / Sent / Failed / On hold (reserve/chargeback)

Filters: provider, date, city, role, bank status, amount ranges

Actions: Retry, Change bank (with KYC check), Split batch for compliance
timing

Failures: display processor reason; allow oneclick retry after method
update

Reserves

Newprovider reserve: first N payouts delayed up to 7 days (config in
§4.14.2)

Rolling reserve: hold X% for Y days; adjustable by cohort/risk
(twoperson approval to change)

Reserve ledger shows applied, released, scheduled releases

Early Payout (peruse)

Eligibility: Rep ≥ 85 + ID Verified (gate in §4.12)

Finance can override eligibility with reason (dualapproval)

Fee: 0.5% on provider fee component only; calculator shows amount before
confirm

Telemetry and reporting on takerate impact

D\) Refunds, Adjustments & Credits

Refunds

Triggered by Support calculators (§4.14.5) or Finance directly (edge
cases)

Autorecompute: provider/buyer fees, taxes, processor costs per policy
matrix; regenerate amended receipt (§4.12)

Partial refunds supported per line (packages, addons, license, travel)

ACH/card: refund path chosen based on original method; if method
expired, create credit memo

Adjustments (Change Orders)

Price deltas (time overage, addon, travel); pushes delta charge to
buyer; fee math applied to delta only

Credits (Goodwill / Ops)

Noncash platform credits with expiration; scoped by city/role; caps in
§4.14.2

Not withdrawable; applied at checkout; tracked as marketing expense

E\) Studio Deposits (Auth Holds & Captures)

Dashboard

Columns: Booking, Host, Amount, Auth expiry, Evidence, Status
(Held/Captured/Void/Released)

Actions

Capture (with evidence attach) → separate deposit receipt (no platform
fee)

Void/Release on resolution or expiry

Twoperson approval if capture \> \$1k or \> default cap in §4.14.2

Rules

Captures must occur before auth expiry; system warns at T24h

Partial capture allowed up to cap; multiple captures if processor
supports incremental
